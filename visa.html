Know Your Visa

Tourist Visa (T)

A tourist visa is a type of visa that allows a foreign national to enter a country for the purpose of tourism.

Business Visa (B)

A business visa allows a foreign national to travel to a country temporarily for business purposes.

Employment Visa (E)

An employment visa allows a foreign national to enter and work in a country for a specific period.
