Frequently asked questions about Goa Tour Packages

The best time to visit Goa is between mid-November to mid-February when the weather is pleasantly cool and comfortable.

Top activities include visiting North Goa beaches, exploring water sports, visiting the Basilica of Bom Jesus, and enjoying the nightlife.

Popular spots include Anjuna Beach, Baga Beach, Calangute, Old Goa churches, and the Dudhsagar Falls.

Palolem and Agonda in South Goa are great for peace, while Baga and Calangute in North Goa are famous for their lively atmosphere.

Goa is world-renowned for its beautiful beaches, Portuguese-influenced architecture, seafood, and vibrant cultural festivals.

Couples can enjoy sunset cruises, romantic beach dinners, long walks at Palolem Beach, and exploring the quiet lanes of Fontainhas.

Flamingo offers a range of packages including South Goa Hotel Deals, North Goa Beach stays, and specialized winter packages.

North Goa is best for nightlife and shopping, while South Goa is ideal for luxury resorts and quiet beaches.
